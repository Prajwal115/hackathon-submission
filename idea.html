Utilizing Carbon-Dioxide

We came up with an idea to utilize the Carbon-Dioxide. Carbon Dioxide is produced as we exale, burning wood, vehicle fumes etc.

CO2 = C + O2

We found that it is possible to split Carbon and Oxygen, but it would require a large amount of energy.

We can utilize the energy from the sun/wind/tides and other sources to achieve this magnificient outcome. The Carbon split from the molecule could be used in making inks and paints. The Oxygen obtained can be used for medical and research purposes.
